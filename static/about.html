What is myBotCoin?

myBotCoin is a self-hosted Bitcoin savings bot. It runs 24/7 on your own private cloud server and trades automatically on the Kraken exchange. You own everything — the server, the Kraken account, the keys. There is no middleman, no subscription, no company holding your funds.

The single mission: accumulate as much Bitcoin as possible over the long term.

We measure success in BTC (satoshis), not USD. A lower BTC price isn't bad news — it means more sats per dollar.

The Philosophy

Bitcoin operates on roughly 4-year cycles tied to its halving events. Every cycle so far has followed a pattern: accumulation → bull run → correction → repeat. Long-term holders who bought consistently through bear markets ended up with the most Bitcoin.

The enemy of wealth building is emotion. People sell at bottoms because it feels right. They buy at tops because everyone else is excited. myBotCoin removes emotion entirely.

💡 The bot buys on a schedule no matter what. It buys harder when prices dip. It doesn't panic. It doesn't get greedy. It just stacks.

How It Works — The Three Engines

① Scheduled DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)

A fixed amount of USD is invested on a set schedule — daily, weekly, or monthly. This is the baseline: consistent accumulation regardless of what the market does. Nobody times the bottom perfectly. DCA smooths everything out.

② Dip Buying

The bot monitors Bitcoin's price around the clock. When price drops a set percentage from its recent high, it automatically deploys part of your USD reserve to buy more sats at a discount. Three tiers — the bigger the dip, the bigger the buy.

③ The Recycler — The Most Powerful Feature

This is the bot playing the market for free money.

  1. The bot watches your BTC position and average cost basis
  2. When BTC rises enough above your cost basis, it sells a portion for USD profit
  3. It waits for BTC to dip back down
  4. It buys back at the lower price
  5. Result: you end up with more BTC than you started — funded by market volatility, not your wallet

💡 These gains are called "Recycler funds" — money the bot earned, not money you deposited. The dashboard shows exactly how much of your stack came from the Recycler vs. your own DCA contributions.

The Flywheel Effect

More BTC → Bigger Recycler pool → More house money → Buys more BTC on dips → Bigger stack → Bigger Recycler pool → ...

The bot is self-funding its own growth. DCA is the fuel to get started. The Recycler is the engine that runs on its own once you have enough of a stack.
